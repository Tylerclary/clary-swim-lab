Who is Tyler?

Tyler Clary is an Olympic Gold Medalist, World Champion, and former American Record Holder who spent over a decade competing at the highest levels of swimming. He captured gold at the 2012 London Olympics in the 200-meter backstroke, setting a new Olympic Record.

Today, Tyler brings the same passion for excellence to Clary Swim Lab, helping swimmers of all levels master technique and maximize their potential. With thousands of athletes coached, Tyler combines elite knowledge with practical, swimmer-friendly instruction to deliver results.
